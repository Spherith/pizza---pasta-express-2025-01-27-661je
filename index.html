About Us

Founded in 2015 by Master Chef Antonio Romano, Pizza & Pasta Express began as a small family-owned restaurant with a simple mission: bringing authentic Italian cuisine to German homes. Our journey started with a single location and a commitment to using only the finest ingredients imported directly from Italy.

Today, we remain dedicated to our core values of quality, authenticity, and customer satisfaction. Our team of passionate chefs and delivery professionals works tirelessly to ensure every meal delivers a true taste of Italy. We believe in sustainable practices, supporting local communities, and creating memorable dining experiences for every customer.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery hours and areas?

We deliver daily from 11:00 AM to 10:00 PM within a 5km radius of our restaurant locations. Delivery times typically range from 25-35 minutes depending on distance and current order volume.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes! We offer vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options. All our menu items are clearly labeled with allergen information, and we can modify most dishes to accommodate specific dietary needs.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and cash on delivery. Online payments are processed through a secure payment gateway.

Do you offer catering services?

Yes, we provide catering services for events of all sizes. Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance for catering orders. Special menu options and bulk pricing are available.

What is your cancellation policy?

Orders can be canceled free of charge within 5 minutes of placing them. After that, cancellation may be subject to a processing fee depending on the order status.
